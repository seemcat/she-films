she films

for girlies in their storytelling era

the origin

she films society was born from a simple truth: we're all struggling with the same thing - creating content but never posting it.

founded by maricris, a content creator who got tired of recording everything and posting nothing, she films is where aspiring creators build the habit of showing up for their storytelling dreams.

what we believe

your stories matter.

not because they'll go viral. not because they'll make you famous. but because they're yours, and the world needs to hear them.

we believe in:

  • aligned storytelling > virality
  • progress > perfection
  • sister energy > competition
  • showing up > waiting to be ready
