6 rules of the
sunday ritual

rule 1: show up every sunday

  • this is the foundation. we meet every sunday at 7am PT for 10 weeks
  • if you can't make it and you let me know ahead of time, we can figure it out
  • if you ghost without saying anything, that tells me you're not ready for this level of commitment

rule 2: one platform, one output habit, one process habit

  • you're not here to do everything. you're here to do ONE thing consistently
  • pick one platform to focus on for the season. you can repost elsewhere, but this is your priority
  • your output habit = what you're posting (ex: 1 youtube video per week)
  • your process habit = how you're making it happen (ex: edit for 1 hour every tuesday at 7pm)

rule 3: post, don't hoard

  • this is our core value. we're not here to plan forever. we're here to ship
  • messy > missing. done > perfect
  • if you're overthinking, you're not posting. and if you're not posting, you're not building the muscle

  • you'll be paired with someone (or you can choose your own)
  • every wednesday, you check in with each other ‚Äî how's it going, did you post, do you need support

rule 5: three strikes and you reset

  • this isn't about punishment. it's about being honest with yourself
  • if your goal is too ambitious and you keep missing, the answer isn't to beat yourself up. it's to make it easier and rebuild the streak
  • the point is to stay in the game. lower the bar if you need to ‚Äî just don't stop showing up
  • if you're struggling, talk to me before you hit 3 strikes. we'll figure it out together

rule 6: bring sister energy

  • we support each other. we hype each other. we don't compete
  • this is a room where you can be vulnerable, share your fears, and celebrate your wins
  • grace + accountability. that's the vibe
